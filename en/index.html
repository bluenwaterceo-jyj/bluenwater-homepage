Core Technology Stack

Our process architecture combines RaDX catalyst materials, Bluechip Media, and the AQUACOM platform for chemical-light, low-energy, and low-maintenance water treatment.

01

RaDX Blue / Red

Catalyst materials for disinfection, odor reduction, decomposition of persistent pollutants, decolorization, and resource recovery.

02

Bluechip Media

PVDC-based high-porosity adsorption media for SS and heavy-metal capture with long replacement cycles.

03

AQUACOM

Advanced Quality Catalytic Oxidation & Media treatment platform for different industrial applications.

04

Blue Eye / AI DX

Roadmap for water-quality data, autonomous process control, predictive maintenance, and AI-based operation.

CEO Message

We create usable water wherever it is needed.

Hello. I am Jung Yoonjae, CEO of BLUE N WATER.

BLUE N WATER is not trying to become a company that simply purifies water. We aim to become a water infrastructure company that helps industries, communities, disaster sites, remote areas, and future extreme environments continue operating without being stopped by water limitations.

Based on catalyst-driven water treatment technologies accumulated over many years, we are building practical solutions for food-processing water reuse, industrial wastewater pretreatment, swimming pool water quality management, and the BLUE BOX mobile water purification system.

BLUE N WATER will continue to apply technology in the field, prove performance with verifiable numbers, and expand sustainable water independence technologies into global markets.

Jung Yoonjae
