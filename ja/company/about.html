CEO Message

水が必要なあらゆる場所で、使える水をつくります。

こんにちは。BLUE N WATER代表取締役の鄭潤在（Yoonjae Jung）です。

BLUE N WATER is not simply trying to become a company that purifies water. We aim to become a water infrastructure company that keeps people, industries, disaster sites, remote areas, and future harsh environments from stopping because of water.

Based on decades of catalyst-driven water treatment know-how, we build practical solutions for food-processing water reuse, industrial wastewater pretreatment, swimming pool water management, and BLUE BOX mobile water treatment systems.

We will continue to apply technology in the field, prove performance with verifiable numbers, and expand sustainable water-independence technology into global markets.

Yoonjae Jung
CEO, BLUE N WATER
